Understanding the Enemy

Your belly fat isn't just stored energy. There are two types: subcutaneous fat (the pinchable stuff) and visceral fat (the dangerous hidden fat).


The quick test: Poke your stomach. If it's soft and jiggly = mostly harmless subcutaneous. If it sticks out hard like a basketball = visceral fat pushing against your organs.
